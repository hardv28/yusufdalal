About

HVK International, as a De Beers Sightholder and a part of the Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC), is a well-known natural diamond manufacturer and a prominent diamantaire. With decades of experience and customer satisfaction, we serve customers worldwide with global offices in Hong Kong, UAE, Belgium, India, and Thailand.

HVK sources high-quality roughs only from Kimberley Process countries and provides clients with beautifully cut and polished diamonds.
